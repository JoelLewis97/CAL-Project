To deliver this objective we have become approved installers of the leading brands of liquid roofing, enabling us to provide a comprehensive and informed service.

Benefits of Liquid Roofing

  • Asbestos roof encapsulation
  • Applied straight over old asphalt or felt roofs
  • Seamless finish, excellent for detailing
  • No need to strip existing roof to upgrade
  • Cold applied with no hot works
  • Cheaper than re-roofing or over-sheeting
  • Resistant to impact damage and foot traffic
  • UV Resilient
  • BBA Approved
  • Insurance backed guarantees

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long will a Liquid Roof last?
    The liquid roofing products we supply have guarantees that range from 5 to 25 years.
  • Where can liquid roofing be used?
    Liquid roofing can be used on virtually any substrate, but it is mainly used on flat or pitched (metal or asbestos) roofs.
  • Can liquid roofing be supplied in different colours?
    Liquid roofing can be supplied in a range of colours.
  • Are liquid roofing systems non-slip?
    Liquid roofing systems are available in a non-slip finish.
  • What is the best liquid roofing system for my project?
    It will be dependent on the type of project, but we will advise as to what system will be most suitable from all the leading brands of liquid roofing.
  • Will I need to insulate my roof?
    Building regulations state that if you are refurbishing more than a third of your roof, the insulation must be upgraded to meet the latest thermal requirements.
