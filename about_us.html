About Us

CAL has been established for 20 years in the supply and installation of industrial flooring including Floor coatings, Anti-slip, Resin coatings, line painting, fast cure MMA resin, Rubber Including low smoke rubber Vinyl flooring, Raised access flooring. We also specialise in liquid applied roofing systems and are approved contractors for 3M. Other works included, concrete repairs, concrete, grinding planning and levelling. Painting and decorating

CAL is also a leading supplier of products to the rail industry including Section 12 product and paints. CAL Contracts Ltd offers a vast range of products and paints for use on a variety of surfaces from Rolling Stock to Platforms, Communal Areas and Building Structures. These products are suitable for use in conjunction with almost all of our product ranges in the Rail and Underground Industries. With industry leading suppliers including Ronacrete, Kingspan Access floors, Tensator, 3M and PPG Sigma Range (formerly Ameron), Bondaglass Voss and many other manufactures the durability of these products is second to none.

We pride ourselves on our knowledge of the materials and technologies available in the Rail and Commercial Flooring industry. We offer a unique and professional service by helping companies and individuals find the correct products & materials for their needs.

During the past 20 years CAL has become known for its friendly and dependable service by many clients including MJ Quinn, London Underground, Cleshar Contracts, KN Group and CPS Rail.
